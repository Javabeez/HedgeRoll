____CONTROLS____
* Space = Jump
* Space(hold) = Longer Jump
* Space(midair) = Dive

____More about the game____
Like Geometry Dash, the hedgehog stays fixed on the x-axis
and jumps to get over or under obstacles. The hedgehog also
explodes into oblivion upon side collision with a wall.
The dive mechanic is inspired by Sonic's homing attack.
(Rated E for Everyone.)
